Paterna Cakes

Ingredients

You will say a paterna cake, how disgusting, almost nobody has tasted this because no one has ever dared to bring it to the consumer market, but today we will learn how to make it.

  • Paterna Seed
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Eggs

Preparation process

To prepare the paterna cake, first we will blend the seeds and then we will put the milk, water and eggs in a single bowl and begin to stir when we have a consistency we will put in a tray and 25 minutes in the oven and enjoy.

This recipe is incredible because it is mysterious and tasty, I recommend that you surprise your family by making one of these delicacies.

Paterna's leaf soup

Ingredients

You are getting tired of making the same soups, today we will learn how to make a healthy and of course delicious soup.

  • Paterna leaves
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Condiments

Preparation process

First we will wash our paterna leaves we will put water to the fire when it is boiling we will add the leaves salt and seasoning to taste wait approximately 10 minutes to be able to lower the fire to enjoy it.

These soups are mostly prescribed by doctors for weak people or for people who suffer from headaches or dizziness to give them more strength.

Paterna seed rice

Ingredients

Traditions pass and pass and so does the food. What do you think if we leave the culture of the same old rice and if we learn to make a rice from Paterna?

  • Paterna Seeds
  • Rice
  • Condiments

Preparation process

We will begin by washing the rice and the pod, let the rice rest and begin to remove the seeds to our paterna, put to sew the rice by pouring water when you see that it is half dry throw the seeds, wait 5 minutes and ready to enjoy.

This wonderful rice can be complemented with chicken meat or anything else since it has nothing in it and is even tasty, so you better not be bad and start sharing this recipe.

Exercise Routine

1.

Today we will do different types of stretching, we will start by lying down on the floor in front of us, lifting the left foot for 10 seconds and the right foot for 10 seconds.

2.

We stand up and stretch one foot back and one foot forward and our hands in front of our face for 10 seconds.

3.

We will look for a box, place it in front of us and do a routine as if we were climbing stairs 10 repetitions for each foot.

4.

We will lie down on the floor looking at the ceiling or the sky and lift both feet at the same time and hold them for 10 seconds.

5.

We stretch and lie down again on the floor on our side and look for a ball and hold it for 15 seconds.
