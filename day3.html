Paterna ice cream

Ingredients

Let's leave the same old flavors and start approving new flavors.

  • Ice
  • Paterna
  • Water
  • Artificial flavor

Preparation process

First remove the white from the paterna and put it in the blender with the ice and blend it, then add the artificial flavor of your preference and put it in the refrigerator and you can enjoy it the next day.

One of the most curious facts of this recipe is that besides being a dessert has many vitamins, so in addition to enjoy them you will be vitaminizing your body.

Paterna juice

Ingredients

This is one of those juices that when you see them you say "wow, that's disgusting" but it's the opposite because the contrast of flavors leaves a very good sensation.

  • Water
  • sugar
  • Paterna

Preparation process

We leave the paterna in water for two days, after two days strain the water, add sugar to taste and put it in the refrigerator (it is recommended to leave it in water for 3 days).

Paterna juice contains great vitamins for the body since it is not processed and is made naturally in El Salvador. 8 out of 10 doctors recommend drinking it.

Strawberry Paterna Smoothie

Ingredients

This smoothie is kind of unique because it resembles other smoothies and shakes but it is delicious just like the others.

  • Strawberry
  • Paterna
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Preparation process

Put the strawberries and the paterna in the blender together with the milk, water and sugar, blend and add sugar to taste if you want to add ice for a better taste.

This smoothie contains a lot of vitamins, since strawberries, like paternal strawberries, are rich in vitamins and have an unequaled flavor.
