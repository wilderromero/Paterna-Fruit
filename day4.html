Chicken with Paterna

Ingredients

Chicken with paternal creation in a small town with enough vitamins and proteins.

  • Chicken
  • Paterna
  • vegetables
  • condiments

Preparation process

We cut the chicken and put it to sew, waiting for it to be soft to add the paternas and vegetables. After we add all that we wait 25 minutes to remove it from the fire and enjoy it.

This soup was created for a sick man who could not eat or drink anything so his family decided to elaborate this recipe.

Paterna's Salad

Ingredients

It's time to get out of the common tomato and lettuce salad, today we will learn how to make a different salad.

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Paterna

Preparation process

Wash the lettuce, tomato and paterna, chop them into small pieces and put them in a single bowl, add salt and lemon to taste.

This recipe is very quick to make and can be served with chicken, meat or fish since it is not a main dish but a dish to be served with everything.
