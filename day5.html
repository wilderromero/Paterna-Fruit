Beans with Paterna

Ingredients

The culture in my country is only to eat beans plain beans or beans with meat but last year a woman said why not add paterna and then she made the recipe Beans with Paterna.

  • Sewn beans
  • paterna

Preparation process

With the beans already sewn we save one step, just cut the paterna pod in small pieces and put it together with the beans and wait for the paternas to soften to be able to enjoy them.

This is one of the richest soups in El Salvador because in addition to taking beans which is an excellent vitamin and for many the favorite food takes paterna which is a great complement to the flavors and of course the most important vitamins.

Beef soup with paterna

Ingredients

The common beef soup has gone out of fashion and today it is almost made with any fruit or vegetable and why not because we make it from Paterna, let's see the ingredients.

  • Beef
  • Vegetables
  • Water
  • Salt
  • condiments
  • Paterna

Preparation process

Wash the lettuce, tomato and paterna, chop them into small pieces and put them in a single bowl, add salt and lemon to taste.

Most of all, one of the benefits of this soup is the vitamin it contains, since it is made with different vegetables and the components it has are strong, so we have a soup rich in flavor and vitamin.

Paterna boiled

Ingredients

This is a simple and easy to make recipe but with a rich flavor and 100% vitamins.

  • Paterna
  • Salt
  • Water

Preparation process

First we put the water to boil then when it is hot we put the paterna pods and wait 10 minutes approximately we put them to dry and we put salt to taste.

Since it is one of the easiest recipes that can exist, it is one of the most recommended recipes when one is recently operated on, since with the vitamins it contains, it will give you more strength.
