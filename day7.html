Paterna with salt and lemon

Ingredients

The best food in El Salvador is about 4 or 5 different types of pupusa but today you will learn how to prepare a different pupusa.

  • Salt
  • Lemon
  • Paterna

Preparation process

We put to cook the paternas in water after 10 minutes we take it out and let them rest we look for a container we put the paternas already in pieces on the plate or wherever and we put salt and lemon to taste.

This recipe is very simple to elaborate but many people love the end result because of the flavor it has to eat it.

Egg with Paterna

Ingredients

The boring scrambled eggs have gone out of fashion, let's put our foot down and make eggs with paterna, an incredible and easy to prepare recipe.

  • Egg
  • Paterna
  • Salt

Preparation process

First add the egg to the pan and begin to beat it, add salt to taste, when we see that it is half cooked add the paterna in small pieces and wait for it to cook and enjoy them.

This recipe can be given to children at breakfast so that they have energy and vitamin in their body in the morning.

Paterna Jelly

Ingredients

You have surely tried countless jellies but I swear or rather I assure you that you have never tasted paterna jelly, let's prepare it.

  • 6 to 8 Paterna pods
  • 300 grams of white sugar.

To prepare the jelly, it is necessary to open all the Paterna pods. Remove their contents, clean and remove the seeds, then leave only the cotton flakes.

Preparation process

Proceed to put in a container all the pulp obtained, and put it over low heat with the sugar, until it is well cooked. Once a sticky consistency is achieved, let it rest, and proceed to consume as jelly or jam Paterna.

And the last step is to enjoy it as a family either on toast or however you prefer to eat it, remember that it is a jelly like the others, only in a new discovery.
