Paterna leaf tea

Ingredients

For our first recipe we will not use the seed of the paternas or the casacara but we will use the leaves of the guama (paterna) trees.
The ingredients are as follows:

  • 1 cup of drinking water.
  • 3 leaves of guama (paterna)

Preparation process

1.Bring water to a boil.

2.Once boiling, add the 3 leaves.

3.Let it steep for 5 minutes.

4.And when the tea is lukewarm, consume.

This tea is recommended by doctors in Mexico as it helps different diseases or for better blood flow.

Paterna Jelly

Ingredients

You have surely tried countless jellies but I swear or rather I assure you that you have never tasted paterna jelly, let's prepare it.

  • 6 to 8 Paterna pods
  • 300 grams of white sugar.

To prepare the jelly, it is necessary to open all the Paterna pods. Remove their contents, clean and remove the seeds, then leave only the cotton flakes.

Preparation process

Proceed to put in a container all the pulp obtained, and put it over low heat with the sugar, until it is well cooked. Once a sticky consistency is achieved, let it rest, and proceed to consume as jelly or jam Paterna.

And the last step is to enjoy it as a family either on toast or however you prefer to eat it, remember that it is a jelly like the others, only in a new discovery./p>

Paterna candy

Ingredients

In Mexico City they are known as the famous green candy and today I'm going to show you how to make it.

  • Paterna pods to taste
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Pot

Preparation process

To prepare this recipe the first thing we have to do is to remove the seeds that bring as that will make sweets, then put the sugar and water to sew and when they have already spent 10 minutes to throw the seeds of paterna and finally put in a mold jelly and put it in the refrigerator to freeze and ready to enjoy.

You can enjoy these sweets either at a party or even when you are feeling unhealthy because paterna seeds contain vitamins and can help to improve your health either when you have a cough or a cold.
